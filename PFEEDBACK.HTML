✍️ Draft Audit Findings

Transform raw audit observations into formal finding write-ups using the five-attribute format (Condition, Criteria, Cause, Effect, Recommendation) aligned with Internal Audit Manual standards.

💡 Tip: Upload a PDF with raw findings, or paste findings directly. The bot will draft them in professional audit report format.
🔄 Mode: Guided Mode — structure your findings
Include all details; the bot will structure it

💡 Five-Attribute Finding Format

  • Condition: What did we find? (The facts)
  • Criteria: What should it be? (Policy, standard, regulation)
  • Cause: Why did it happen? (Root cause)
  • Effect: What is the impact? (Risk, financial, compliance)
  • Recommendation: What should be done? (Specific, actionable)

💡 Tips for Control Gap Assessment

  • Upload 2–3 policy documents (PDFs) for comparison
  • Be specific about which control areas matter: "segregation of duties," "approval authority"
  • Specify desired output format: "table," "numbered list," "matrix"
